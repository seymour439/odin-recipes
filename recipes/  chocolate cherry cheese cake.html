Chocolate-Cherry-Cheese-Cake

Description

This Chocolate-Cherry-Cheese-Cake is a decadent dessert that combines the rich flavors of chocolate, the tartness of cherries, and the creamy texture of cheesecake. It's perfect for any occasion and is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

It is pretty easy, but waiting until it is chilled before eating is hard to do! The chilling step is an important one, as the texture and flavor really benefit from time in the fridge. Dark chocolate and cherry are a match made in heaven, in my humble opinion

Ingredients

  • 24 whole chocolate sandwich cookies (such as OREO)
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 pinches salt, divided, or to taste
  • 4 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream, divided
  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling, divided
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
