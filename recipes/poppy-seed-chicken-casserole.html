Poppy-Seed-Chicken-Casserole

Description

This poppy seed chicken casserole is very simple to make with shredded chicken and an easy creamy sauce. You can use leftover cooked chicken if you have it.

Why you’ll Love This Recipe: This recipe layers shredded chicken and a creamy sauce for a comforting, family-style dish. Home cooks praise the buttery cracker and poppy seed topping for a crunchy texture. Allrecipes member Roseanne Alexander explains, "We love to serve this over white rice!"

Ingredients

    4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
    1 sleeve buttery round crackers (such as Ritz), crushed
    1/2 cup butter, melted
    1 teaspoon poppy seeds, or more if desired
    1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
    1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
    2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
